  1. Incantation

    2. This Netflix's lastest movie is a Kevin ko's slow burn Taiwanese found footage horror film. This movie comes from the roots of Taiwan.It has so much going for it.Even the fact that this is actually a "found footage"movie works.It is Really good! What one can call Eerie,Creepy and Bloody at the same time. This film mainly concentrates on the traditions of a curse that gets passed on,ancient rituals performed in a remote region of th mountains,a recurring symbol and an eerie invocation to a faceless deity So,if you are into a mood to watch something refreshing in horror this is the movie to go for undoubtedly. RATE:8/10
